Our Story

In the heart of a bustling school, a young man named Awan found himself struggling with his studies. His grades were slipping, and he felt lost amidst the sea of knowledge. That’s when he met her - a girl named Cery. Cery was a bright student, known for her intellect and kindness. She noticed Awan’s struggles and offered to help him. Awan, initially hesitant, agreed to accept her assistance. Their study sessions started in the school library, amidst the quiet rustle of turning pages and hushed whispers. As time passed, Awan found himself not only improving in his studies but also looking forward to their study sessions. Cery’s patience and understanding made learning enjoyable. He admired her passion for knowledge and her dedication to helping him. Cery, on the other hand, found herself drawn to Awan’s determination to improve. His gratitude and respect towards her were evident in his actions. She admired his resilience and his willingness to learn. Their shared moments in the library soon turned into shared lunches, shared laughter, and shared secrets. Amidst the academic pressure and school chaos, they found solace in each other’s company.
